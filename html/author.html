F.Albuquerque was born in Brazil.

As a kid, he was crazy about super heroes and comics. He had some spiritual journeys playing and creating new adventures for his action figures.

Some years later he kept the same passion working as graphic designer as it came out to be his full time job.

Today he lives in Paris and feel the same passion creating his novels. FAlbuquerque is inspired everyday by many of his favorite tales from JK Rowling, JRR Tolkien and CS Lewis.

The Ellin Terwen © saga, is a result of a long and incredible research about brazilian and foreign folklore, greek mythology and theology.
